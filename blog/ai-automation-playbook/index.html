AI Automation Playbook For Startup Teams

Start with one painful, repetitive workflow. Define success metrics before implementation. Add human checkpoints where mistakes are costly.

Step 1: Workflow Selection

Choose a process with high frequency, clear structure, and measurable output quality.

Step 2: Baseline Metrics

Track time spent, error rate, and throughput before automation to quantify true impact.

Step 3: Controlled Rollout

Ship to a small segment first, review failures, and iterate prompts/rules before full deployment.

Step 4: Continuous QA

Use event logs and exception reporting so your automation quality improves, not degrades, over time.
